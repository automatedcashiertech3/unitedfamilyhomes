United Family Homes has built 5 Single Family Homes. An enclave of five bespoke homes in Buckhead. Situated on one of Atlanta’s most renowned thoroughfares, Brixton Buckhead captures the ease of access to I-75/85 and 400. Its central location makes it a short commute in any direction. The hilltop site of Brixton offers glimpses of the Buckhead skyline and respite from the hustle and bustle of the city — the best of both worlds without compromise. Purposefully designed floors plans offer ample opportunity to work, study, or relax in an area all your own with architectural details that respond to our ever evolving demands.
