United Family Homes has built 30 townhomes and 7 single family homes. Luxury new construction in Downtown Norcross. Loaded with upgrades standard! Granite or quartz in kitchen and ALL baths, 42" kitchen cabinets with upper cabinets to ceiling, under cabinet lighting, stainless farmhouse sink, stainless steel cooktop, oven & microwave. Walking distance to active downtown area with shops, restaurants, parks, concert series, seasonal festivals & more!
