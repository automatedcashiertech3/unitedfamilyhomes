About Us

United Family Homes, LLC was founded in 2005 and the office is located in Duluth, GA. We have built our reputation around fine craftsmanship and specialize in building Custom homes and commercials. For over 12 years, our homes have been designed and built with a focus on quality and value.

  • Producing Liveable Floor Plans
  • Building Energy Efficient Features
  • Offering Home Warranty

We reward your trust in building a new home with United Family Homes by keeping you informed and involved throughout the construction process. From pouring the slab to final checkpoints and orientation, you play a key role in the progress of your home with ample opportunities to provide input.
